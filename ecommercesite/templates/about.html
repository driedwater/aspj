About Us

Company History

The Boutique was established in 1987 with the first physical store located in Singapore, Ang Mo Kio. The Boutique has more than 30 years of experience of manufacturing and selling clothings dedicated to women. Ranging from gowns, dresses to undergarments in various sizes, we offer a large variety of appearals to our beloved customers. Our legacy of craftsmanship and style spans more than 30 years and our products are made with pride since day one.

Today, The Boutique remains committed to the early principles of the company -- great quality, clothings does not need to be expensive and should be available to everyone. A visit to our store physically or digitally and you’ll see just how committed we are to that idea.

Mission & Vision

At The Boutique, our designs are inspired by how you can better experience your family at home. Our designs center not just around the “me” of self-expression, but also the “we” of a home. We hope our designs will facilitate conversations, inspire celebrations or simply help people to create a safe space where they can be themselves.

Our designs are also inspired by the greater home we call Earth. Each of our designs ensure that we are able to maximise the usage of every single wooden log, so that no resources are being wasted.
